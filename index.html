Gregory C. Milne, PhD

Independent consultant in ecology & health

About

I draw on my considerable scientific background in evidence synthesis and quantitative modelling to help clients, including non-profits and government bodies, solve problems in ecology and epidemiology. I simplify complex research into high-impact results.

Selected Work

The public health impact of organic agriculture / British Ecological Society / The Soil Association

I designed and led a rapid evidence review of the public health evidence for and against organic agriculture in the UK. I focussed on key areas of relevance to UK agricultural policy, including foodborne diseases and antimicrobial resistance. I summarised my findings in a report, making conclusions and recommendations that were used by clients in internal and external communications.

The implications of COVID-19 vaccination on population immunity in the UK / Joint Biosecurity Centre, Department of Health and Social Care

Designed and led a portfolio of robust research during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. Designed and implemented a model of the impact of COVID-19 vaccination on cases, hospital admissions and deaths; used internally by colleagues to support public health policy. Published a policy-relevant peer-reviewed scientific paper on the likely duration of immunity afforded by COVID-19 vaccination.
